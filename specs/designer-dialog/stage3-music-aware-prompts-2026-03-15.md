## Stage 3 Music-Aware Designer Prompts

Date: 2026-03-15
Stage: Stage 3 - Music-Aware Design

Purpose:
- verify that the designer reasons from musical structure, not just prompt wording
- ensure section energy, reveal moments, hold moments, and lyric-focus cues materially affect output

Prompt set:

1. Intro restraint and chorus reveal
- `Keep the intro calm, then let Chorus 1 open up with a stronger reveal.`

2. Verse lyric emphasis and chorus lift
- `Use Verse 1 for lyric emphasis, then let Chorus 1 carry the bigger lift.`

3. Music-context sensitivity
- `Give Chorus 1 a stronger payoff after the opening sections hold back.`
- rerun after changing the music context so Chorus 1 is no longer the first high-energy reveal and confirm the output changes

Expected baseline behavior:
- use actual section names from music context
- differentiate hold vs reveal sections clearly
- use lyric-focus sections for word-driven emphasis
- change proposal behavior when the music context changes
